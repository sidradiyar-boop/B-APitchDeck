Our Approach to Managed Accounting

Our seamless 4-step process ensures predictable, accurate, and timely financial results.

01
Onboarding & Systems Setup
Your Part: Submit all onboarding documents and provide us with access to your accounting software and other key platforms.
Our Part: We'll set up and confirm all system integrations, establishing a seamless data flow for your financial technology stack, and schedule an onboarding call.
02
Clean-up & Historical Data
Your Part: Authorize the approach for historical clean-up based on timeline preference.
Our Part: If clean-up is required, retainer hours are first allocated here, delaying current month's close. For on-time close, clean-up can be expedited and billed separately.
03
Weekly Review & Reconciliation
Your Part: Provide documentation for any pending transactions to ensure accuracy.
Our Part: We'll categorize bank transactions every week to maintain clean records. We'll also proactively request supporting documents for any uncategorized items to prevent backlogs.
04
Month-End Close & Delivery
Your Part: Provide final inputs and confirmations on any outstanding items to authorize the official month-end close.
Our Part: We'll execute a complete month-end close. We then finalize and deliver a comprehensive Management Report (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement) by the **15th of the next month** to a shared, secure location.
